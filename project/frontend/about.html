Our Story

From a Vision to Tanzania's Trusted Builder

Pyramid Engineering and Construction LTD was founded in 2008 with a single mission: deliver exceptional construction services with transparency, precision, and pride. Starting with residential projects in Dar es Salaam, we rapidly grew into one of Tanzania's most recognized engineering firms.

Today, we serve individual homebuilders, corporate clients, and government institutions across Tanzania, bringing the same commitment to quality and accountability to every project — regardless of size or scope.

Our name reflects our philosophy: like a pyramid, every project we build stands on a strong foundation, rises with precision, and endures for generations.

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Year by Year

Key milestones that shaped who we are today.

2008
Company Founded
Pyramid Engineering was established in Dar es Salaam with a team of 8 engineers, focusing on residential construction in Kinondoni and Ilala districts.
2012
First Government Contract
Awarded our first major government infrastructure project — a 2km road rehabilitation project in Dodoma, cementing our credibility in public-sector work.
2015
Architecture Division Launched
Expanded services to include full Architecture Planning & Design, growing our team to 30+ professionals and opening a dedicated design studio.
2019
100th Project Milestone
Celebrated the completion of our 100th project — a 6-storey commercial complex in Kariakoo — marking a decade of sustainable growth.
2023
ISO Quality Certification
Received ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification, reinforcing our commitment to international construction standards.
2025
300+ Projects Completed
Surpassed 300 completed projects across Tanzania, with an expanding portfolio of luxury residential, commercial, and government infrastructure developments.
