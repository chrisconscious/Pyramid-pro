Home Projects Kariakoo Commercial Plaza

Project Overview

The Kariakoo Commercial Plaza stands as one of Pyramid Engineering and Construction LTD's most ambitious commercial projects in Dar es Salaam. Commissioned by a private investment consortium, this 8-story mixed-use development combines retail ground-floor units, office floors, and a rooftop event venue — all designed to serve the vibrant Kariakoo business district.

Our team delivered full turnkey services: architectural planning and design, structural engineering, interior fit-out coordination, and MEP systems installation. The project demanded precision in foundation work given the high water table of the area, which our engineers addressed with deep bored piling and a reinforced raft foundation.

The facade features a contemporary curtain-wall glass system with an aluminium composite cladding accent band, chosen for both durability in Tanzania's coastal climate and the sleek, professional impression befitting a premium business address.
